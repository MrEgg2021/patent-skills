## Sorting

Source level: `official_help_doc`

- Google Patents search results default to `Relevance`.
- Alternative sort views exist for filing-date ordering.

Source level: `live_ui_observation_2026-03-17`

- The live control label on 2026-03-17 was `Sort by · Relevance`.

## Semantic-like features

Source level: `official_help_doc`

- Prior Art Finder accepts a larger text passage and extracts search terms.
- Similar Documents uses text similarity against the open patent document.
- Google Patents can expand some queries with close synonyms and inflectional forms.

## Internal wording rule

Source level: `internal_design_rule`

- Describe this as `semantic-enhanced behavior` or `semantic-like assistance`.
- Do not describe Google Patents as exposing a standalone official `semantic search` product feature.
